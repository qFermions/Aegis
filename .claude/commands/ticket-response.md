## Jira Ticket Response — Draft

**Ask me:**
1. What's the ticket about? (brief description of the issue)
2. What's the status? (investigating, resolved, waiting on user, waiting on vendor, needs more info)
3. What tone? (formal / friendly-professional / quick update) — default: friendly-professional

---

**Common ticket reply templates:**

**Acknowledging a new ticket:**
> Hi there — I've received your ticket and I'm looking into it now. I'll update you shortly with next steps.

**Update — still investigating:**
> Quick update: I'm actively working on this. I've [brief action taken] and I'm waiting on [next step]. I'll follow up by [time/date].

**Resolved:**
> This has been resolved. Here's what was done: [brief explanation]. Please let me know if you run into any further issues and I'll be happy to help.

**Waiting on user:**
> I need a bit more info to move forward. Could you confirm: [specific question]? Once I have that I can get this sorted for you.

**Waiting on vendor:**
> I've escalated this to [vendor] and I'm waiting on their response. Case #: [#]. I'll update you as soon as I hear back.

**Closing without resolution (no response):**
> I'm going to close this ticket as I haven't heard back. If you're still experiencing this issue, please reopen or submit a new ticket and I'll take another look.

Tell me the situation and I'll write the full reply.
