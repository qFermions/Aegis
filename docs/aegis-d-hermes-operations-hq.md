## When Aegis should use Hermes

Escalate through `/ask-hermes` when the task benefits from broader context:

- Cross-domain risk: IT + finance/legal/executive impact.
- Architecture/design review before a production change.
- Cybersecurity interpretation beyond a simple admin-center workflow.
- Vendor, business, or stakeholder communication where tone matters.
- War Room, markets, macro, portfolio, or investment-research questions.
- Ambiguous root-cause problems where a second model may catch the missing edge.

Do **not** escalate routine IT tickets that Aegis can solve directly.
